# PawLens Build Week Demo Deck

## Title

PawLens — Turn Uncertainty into Better Observation

## Audience and context

- **Audience:** OpenAI Build Week 2026 judges
- **Track:** Apps for Your Life
- **Context:** A recorded product demonstration submitted with the Devpost entry

## Core message

PawLens does not claim to translate a dog's inner state. It turns a guardian's factual description into a structured, non-diagnostic observation guide and one calm next action.

## Sections and key messages

1. **Problem:** Guardians need a safe alternative to jumping from “something feels different” to a label.
2. **Product:** PawLens makes observation conversational inside ChatGPT.
3. **Live workflow:** Open the widget, establish context, assess a visitor or doorbell reaction, and choose a calm next step.
4. **Trust:** Separate provisional model possibilities from owner-confirmed facts; do not diagnose.
5. **Technology:** GPT-5.6 supplies structured reasoning; Codex accelerated the MCP app, tests, and session-recovery work.
6. **Close:** Judges can try the public MCP endpoint in ChatGPT Developer Mode.
