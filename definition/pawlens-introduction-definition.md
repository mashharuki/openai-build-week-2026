# PawLens Introduction Section

## Purpose

Open the PawLens demo by explaining what PawLens is, why it was created, and the product boundary that makes it different from a dog “translation” or diagnosis app.

## Audience and constraints

- OpenAI Build Week judges
- Four English slides, approximately 45 seconds
- Japanese speaker notes
- No supplied ChatGPT screenshots
- Use the existing PawLens background asset and original text-based visuals

## Narrative

1. **Why PawLens:** everyday dog signals create uncertainty, not a reliable diagnosis.
2. **The gap:** guardians have fragments of observation but need a calm, actionable way to understand what to notice next.
3. **The principle:** do not translate the dog or present a label as fact; distinguish possibilities from confirmed observations.
4. **The bridge:** PawLens turns a factual description into a structured observation guide, which the following demo will show.
